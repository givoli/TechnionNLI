Instructions

Suppose you have an assistant, and you want them to carry out some given task for you. So you need to give your assistant a suitable instruction.

  • You will be given a description of a task that needs to be done.
  • You'll write an instruction for your assistant accordingly. Your instruction should be a single English imperative sentence that a person is likely to say to their assistant given the task they want their assistant to perform.
  • Please make sure your instruction is unambiguous, but also try to make it short (as you would in real life). It must not be more than one sentence.
  • Your instruction shouldn't contain irrelevant information or unnecessary phrases (e.g. the word "please").

 

Example

Suppose you have 4 boxes (each is either red, green or blue), and you want to change the way they are positioned in your room. The following figure defines the task. The left column shows the current position of the boxes, and the right column shows the desired position of the boxes.
